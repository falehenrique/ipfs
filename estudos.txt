IPFS serves content, but content can be much wider than just a pdf-file.
Application logic is content as well. In combination with other decentralized projects like Ethereum,
IPFS is extremely powerful as well. A blockchain like Ethereum can then serve as the back-end of your App, while the front-end is served by IPFS.
This makes for a completely decentralized application.
